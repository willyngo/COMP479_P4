As a result, all shuttle bus service is cancelled until the university reopens . For more information, please see Concordia’s COVID-19 updates .            Free shuttle between campuses  The Concordia shuttle bus offers you a free ride between the SGW and Loyola Campuses.   Shuttle buses run from 7:45 a.m. to 7:45 p.m., Monday through Friday  The ride is approximately 30 minutes  Buses leave at least once or twice every hour. You can find the bus schedule and arrival/departure points on this webpage when it is operational again  You’ll need your Concordia ID card to board the shuttle  Buses are wheelchair accessible , except for replacement buses  Departure and travel times can vary due to unexpected circumstances, like traffic and/or weather                            Campus bus stops      Henry F. Hall Building front doors, 1455 De Maisonneuve Blvd. W., SGW Campus  Loyola Chapel , 7137 Sherbrooke St. W., Loyola Campus               Shuttle bus rules   Valid Concordia ID cards required.  Children accompanied by an adult with a valid Concordia ID are allowed.  No food. Any beverage must be in a travel mug.  Alcohol is strictly forbidden.  In case of an emergency, follow the driver’s instructions.           Back to top    © Concordia University
